Про нашу клинику

Featured services

At Sana Clinic, we pride ourselves on having a dedicated team of highly skilled and compassionate doctors who are committed to providing exceptional healthcare services to our patients.

Наша команда врачей Клиники "Сана" гордится тем, что в нашем распоряжении – это команда высококвалифицированных и отзывчивых специалистов, которые готовы предоставить выдающиеся медицинские услуги нашим пациентам.

Clinic Services / Услуги клиники

Primary Care / Первичная медицинская помощь

Preventive healthcare, routine check-ups, vaccinations, and managing common medical conditions.

Telemedicine Services / Телемедицинские услуги

Telemedicine consultations for remote access to healthcare via video or phone appointments, promoting accessibility and convenience.

Diagnostic Imaging / Диагностическое изображение

X-rays, ultrasounds, CT scans, and MRI scans for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

Laboratory Services / Лабораторные исследования

On-site laboratory for timely and accurate diagnostic testing including blood tests, urine analysis, microbiology, and pathology services.

Emergency Care / Скорая помощь

Dedicated emergency department for prompt and efficient handling of medical emergencies including initial stabilization, assessment, and appropriate treatment interventions.

Wellness Programs / Программы здоровья

Wellness programs focused on preventive health measures, lifestyle modifications, nutrition counseling, and stress management techniques for holistic well-being.

Chronic Disease Management / Управление хроническими заболеваниями

Comprehensive management plans for chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and arthritis aimed at optimizing health outcomes and improving quality of life.

Specialty Care / Специализированная медицинская помощь

Specialized medical services including cardiology, dermatology, gastroenterology, obstetrics and gynecology, orthopedics, pediatrics, and more.

Surgical Services / Хирургические услуги

Wide range of surgical procedures from minor outpatient surgeries to complex interventions performed by skilled surgical teams in modern operating theaters.

Rehabilitation Services / Реабилитационные услуги

Physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and specialized rehabilitation programs for recovery following surgery or injury, tailored to individual needs.
